Christianity, the most widely practiced religion in the world, first appeared around 2000 years ago. It is a monotheistic faith, and they believe in the concept of the father, the son, and the Holy Spirit. Christians believe that Jesus of Nazareth was the Messiah and was crucified to save them.
The Bible is the holy scripture of Christianity. Some important Christian holidays are Christmas and Easter.
There are many denominations of Christianity, with Orthodox, Catholic, and Protestant branches of the church all existing. These branches themselves have branches, such as Protestantism, which has denominations like Methodist, Presbyterian, and Baptist. Christianity is the dominant religion in Europe, North and South America, Australia, Sub-Saharan Africa, and the Philippines.
