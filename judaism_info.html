Judaism dates back to almost 4000 years ago and is one of the oldest still-existing religions. Jewish people believe in one God, and they believe that the Messiah has not come yet but will in the future. Judaism, according to its texts, is founded by a man named Abraham.
Most Jewish people currently live in Israel or in the United States. There are around 14 million Jews globally.
The Torah is a Jewish sacred text, and it is also called the Tanakh or the Hebrew Bible.
